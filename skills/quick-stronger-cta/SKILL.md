{{brand.info}}

## RULES

- 2-4 words per CTA.
- Start with a verb. "Shop the Sale", "Grab a Bag", "Finish Checkout".
- Match the outcome the click delivers. A CTA above a product card should name the action ("Add to Cart"), not the destination ("Learn More").
- Vary CTAs across blocks — don't repeat the same words.
- Keep brand voice. A conversational brand keeps lowercase ("grab them"); a bold brand stays bold.
- Return the full rewritten email, not a diff.
