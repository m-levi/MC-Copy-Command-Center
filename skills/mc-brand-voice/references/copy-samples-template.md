  client to confirm they're on-brand, then capture those as the starter set.
- Mix of campaign + flow types (avoid all being one kind)
- Every sample annotated with "why this works"
- Optional "anti-version" contrast to sharpen the lesson

WHEN TO ADD NEW SAMPLES:
- A send notably outperformed (open rate, CTR, revenue)
- A send got explicit client praise (Michal/Noach on Adina, Swag on FatSwag)
- A send demonstrates a voice move that wasn't yet captured

WHEN TO RETIRE OLD SAMPLES:
- The brand has evolved past that tone
- A newer send does the same job better
- The client has explicitly rejected that direction
