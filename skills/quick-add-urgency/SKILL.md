## RULES

- Use truthful urgency: real deadlines, real stock levels, real launch windows. Never fabricate numbers.
- Prefer specifics ("Ends Sunday at midnight", "200 bags, no restock") over vague FOMO ("Don't miss out!").
- Work urgency into the hero subhead and the CTA block — not every paragraph.
- Keep brand voice. A calm brand stays calm; a punchy brand gets punchier.
- Return the full rewritten email, not a diff.
